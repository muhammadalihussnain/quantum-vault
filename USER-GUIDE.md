## Getting Started

### What is Quantum Vault?

Quantum Vault is a secure messaging application that uses cutting-edge cryptography to protect your conversations. Unlike traditional messaging apps, Quantum Vault uses:

- **End-to-End Encryption:** Only you and your recipient can read messages
- **Quantum Random Numbers:** True randomness from quantum physics
- **Post-Quantum Cryptography:** Protection against future quantum computers
- **Real-time Messaging:** Instant delivery with typing indicators

### System Requirements

**Web Browser:**
- Chrome 90+ (recommended)
- Firefox 88+
- Safari 14+
- Edge 90+

**Internet Connection:**
- Minimum: 1 Mbps
- Recommended: 5 Mbps or higher

## Creating an Account

### Step 1: Navigate to Registration

1. Open your web browser
2. Go to `https://quantum-vault.example.com`
3. Click "Register" or "Sign Up"

### Step 2: Fill Registration Form

**Username:**
- 3-30 characters
- Letters, numbers, underscores only
- Must be unique

**Email:**
- Valid email address
- Used for account recovery
- Must be unique

**Password Requirements:**
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
- At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
- At least one number (0-9)
- At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

**Example Strong Password:**
```
MySecure2024!Pass
```

### Step 3: Complete Registration

1. Enter your username
2. Enter your email
3. Create a strong password
4. Confirm your password
5. Click "Register"

### Step 4: Automatic Login

After successful registration, you'll be automatically logged in and redirected to your dashboard.

## Security Features

### End-to-End Encryption

**What it means:**
- Messages are encrypted on your device
- Only you and recipient can read them
- Not even Quantum Vault servers can read them

**How it works:**
1. Your device generates encryption keys
2. Keys are exchanged securely
3. Messages are encrypted before sending
4. Recipient's device decrypts messages

### Quantum Random Numbers

**What it means:**
- Uses true randomness from quantum physics
- More secure than computer-generated randomness
- Powered by Australian National University QRNG

**Where it's used:**
- Encryption key generation
- Message encryption
- Session tokens

### Post-Quantum Cryptography

**What it means:**
- Protected against future quantum computers
- Uses NIST-approved algorithms
- Hybrid approach (classical + quantum-resistant)

**Algorithms used:**
- ML-KEM-768 (Kyber): Key exchange
- ML-DSA-65 (Dilithium): Digital signatures
- ECC P-256: Classical encryption
- AES-GCM-256: Message encryption

### Security Indicators

**Encrypted Conversation:**
- üîí Lock icon next to username
- Green "Encrypted" badge

**Verified User:**
- ‚úì Checkmark next to username
- Blue "Verified" badge

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is Quantum Vault free?**  
A: Yes, Quantum Vault is currently free to use.

**Q: How secure is Quantum Vault?**  
A: Quantum Vault uses military-grade encryption with quantum-resistant algorithms. Your messages are end-to-end encrypted and cannot be read by anyone except you and your recipient.

**Q: Can Quantum Vault read my messages?**  
A: No. Messages are encrypted on your device before being sent. We cannot decrypt or read your messages.

**Q: What happens if I forget my password?**  
A: Use the "Forgot Password" feature to reset it via email. Note that we cannot recover your password as it's encrypted.

**Q: Can I use Quantum Vault on mobile?**  
A: Mobile apps are coming soon! Currently, use the web version on your mobile browser.

### Security Questions

**Q: What is end-to-end encryption?**  
A: It means messages are encrypted on your device and can only be decrypted by the recipient. No one in between (including us) can read them.

**Q: What is post-quantum cryptography?**  
A: It's encryption that remains secure even against future quantum computers, which could break current encryption methods.

**Q: Are my messages stored on servers?**  
A: Messages are stored encrypted on servers for delivery. Once delivered and read, they can be deleted.

**Q: Can someone intercept my messages?**  
A: No. Messages are encrypted end-to-end. Even if intercepted, they cannot be decrypted without your private keys.

**Q: What are quantum random numbers?**  
A: They're truly random numbers generated using quantum physics, making encryption more secure than computer-generated randomness.

### Account Questions

**Q: Can I change my username?**  
A: Currently, usernames cannot be changed. This feature may be added in the future.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**  
A: Go to Profile ‚Üí Account Settings ‚Üí Deactivate Account. Contact support for permanent deletion.

**Q: Can I recover deleted messages?**  
A: No. Deleted messages are permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

**Q: How many devices can I use?**  
A: You can log in on multiple devices. View and manage them in Profile ‚Üí Active Sessions.

### Feature Questions

**Q: Can I send files?**  
A: File sharing is coming soon in a future update.

**Q: Can I make voice/video calls?**  
A: Voice and video calls are planned for future releases.

**Q: Can I create group chats?**

**Q: Can I customize the theme?**

## Glossary

**End-to-End Encryption (E2EE):** Encryption where only sender and recipient can read messages.

**Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC):** Encryption resistant to quantum computer attacks.

**Quantum Random Number Generator (QRNG):** Device that generates truly random numbers using quantum physics.

**ECC (Elliptic Curve Cryptography):** Modern encryption using elliptic curves.

**AES-GCM:** Advanced Encryption Standard in Galois/Counter Mode.

**JWT (JSON Web Token):** Secure way to transmit information between parties.

**WebSocket:** Protocol for real-time bidirectional communication.
